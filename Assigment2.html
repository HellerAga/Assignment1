System Introduction:

  • Conetxt: Sowder Library is an academic library affiliated with Laurel Ridge Community College.
  • Users: LRCC students (e.g., young adult and adult students, veterans), dual enrollment students, Mountain Vista Governor’s School students, and non-student community members who reside within its service region boundaries.
  • Content: This institution primarily aims to support curriculum and instruction programs for students and faculty. Sowder’s information system provides databases (over 160), collections, books, e-books, journals, magazines, streaming video collections, and sound recordings. The system delivers to its users, i.e., faculty links with different resources ( e.g., research guides) to supplement Canvas courses research guides, databases, articles, and books.

My discussion in the system

  • Navigation: Consistent, it is easy to find information resources.
  • Efficiency: Response time is fast. Only a few clicks to get to the source of information.
  • Design: The background is soft white, and the fonts are easy to read. Interactive links are light blue.
  • Colorful photos of newly added books link users with their location in the library database.
  • Hyperlinks usage.
  • Embedded links to the websites, e.g., YouTube and Google Maps.

Overall, the system is user-friendly, not overcrowded, and provides access to comprehensive information resources.
